Who We Are

Local, Trusted,
Detail-Obsessed.

Dastrup Deep Cleaning was founded with one simple goal: to give Utah families and businesses a cleaning service they could actually rely on. We show up on time, we do the job right, and we don't leave until you're satisfied.

We've built our reputation one clean home at a time — not through advertising, but through word of mouth from clients who couldn't believe the difference a truly thorough clean could make. Today, hundreds of Utah homeowners and businesses trust us on a recurring basis.

We're proudly local, fully insured, and committed to delivering a level of care that national franchises simply can't match.
